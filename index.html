Visual language

How the page should see him

Silver hair, charcoal skin, pale eyes, and a silhouette defined more by stillness than bulk. The look is severe enough to become beautiful, and beautiful enough to make the danger feel chosen.

Reference pillars

  • Kaz Brekker for strategic concealment and charm as a weapon.
  • Fenris for the marked survivor carrying power touched by systems of harm.
  • Alucard for elegance, stillness, and cultivated danger.
  • Zuko for the late-story turn toward chosen advocacy.
  • Daud for methodology, room-reading, and becoming something more than the original role.
